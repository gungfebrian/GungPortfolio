# The Mission

As a dedicated hybrid athlete and computer engineering lead, I recognized that standard fitness applications often fail to capture the nuance of body weight exercises. Most trackers rely on simple sensors that miss subtle errors in posture and technique. 

I engineered **T Moves** to bridge this gap, turning a smartphone into a dedicated AI coach that validates the integrity of every rep. Through rigorous testing, I successfully reduced false positives and improved detection reliability to help users reach their peak physical potential through precise data.

The core of this project is a sophisticated movement detection system that delivers a **thirty percent increase in tracking performance**. By migrating from basic upper body tracking to a full body model, the system now analyzes twelve critical body landmarks simultaneously.

### The Vision Engine
The application processes a live video feed to extract twelve critical body landmarks. Unlike basic trackers that only see the upper body, T Moves analyzes the entire human frame from head to toe. This allows the system to distinguish between a genuine pull up and mere momentum or swinging.

### Performance Achievements
* **Accuracy Optimization**: Boosted detection accuracy from sixty percent to **ninety percent** by implementing full body movement validation.
* **Feature Expansion**: Increased the validation framework from four basic checks to **seven comprehensive biomechanical conditions**.
* **Reliability**: Improved detection in varied lighting by optimizing confidence thresholds from seventy percent down to a more robust **fifty percent**.
* **System Intelligence**: Expanded landmark tracking by **one hundred percent**, moving from six upper body points to twelve full body points.

## Challenges

Building T Moves required solving complex problems at the intersection of hardware and software.

1. **The Precision Balance**: Initial tests were too strict and caused the app to miss valid reps. I recalibrated the arm angle and confidence thresholds to create a system that is both forgiving for natural movement and rigorous for form quality.
2. **Eliminating Motion Noise**: To prevent the app from counting simple arm swings, I developed a temporal tracking algorithm that ensures the app only counts a rep when it detects the entire body moving vertically in unison.
3. **Data Flow Optimization**: Processing high resolution pose data can lag on mobile devices. I optimized the data flow to maintain a smooth user experience while performing heavy computational checks.
