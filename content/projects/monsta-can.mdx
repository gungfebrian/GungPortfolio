## ‚öôÔ∏è Engineering The Logic

The system relies on a **Dual-Sensor Architecture** to automate the workflow seamlessly.

### 1. Proximity Automation (The Reflex)
The first ultrasonic sensor acts as the robot's "eyes."
* **Detection:** Continuously scans the frontal area for user presence.
* **Action:** When you approach (within defined threshold), the ESP32 triggers the servo to lift the lid instantly. No touch required.

### 2. Gamification Engine (The Scorekeeper)
The second sensor is mounted inside the chassis.
* **Tracking:** It monitors the internal volume. When an object passes through the opening, it registers a "drop."
* **Logic:** The system increments the global counter, turning a chore into a high-score chase.

Control should be absolute and accessible. I hosted a dedicated **Web Server** directly on the ESP32, allowing any device on the network to access the dashboard.

* **Live Status:** Instantly view if the lid is `LOCKED` or `OPEN`.
* **Real-Time Analytics:** Track your "Daily Drops" or total trash count with zero latency.
